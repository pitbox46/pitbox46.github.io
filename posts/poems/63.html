Lay

January 11, 2023

I don't typically lay on this side to write. I have my right hand holding my head As if it's some grand prize of importance. I Will Follow You into the Dark is playing But only through half my skull. Ben's voice is much too soft to penetrate The grey matter of my mind. The melody is so beautiful That I can no longer understand it. Maybe if I lay the other way I could hear the notes from my heart. His voice could sink into the other half. Maybe if I lay the other way.
