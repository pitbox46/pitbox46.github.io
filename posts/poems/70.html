mice

January 29, 2023 - Edited March 3, 2023

The taste of your lips lingers like the rancid smell of alcohol... which was spilled, unknowingly on my deep sea green carpet which was not there the first time you knew me... then, I shot mice for only fun or maybe I shot them to feel... something other than your sweet love that cradled and suffocated my poor young heart of apathy... sometimes I feel bad for those mice how I looked them in their deep eyes... while I played as all knowing god who could not be bothered with you and your intoxicating eyes... i fell, discreetly like the mice falling for clever, lethal traps... which snapped loudly in silent nights and punished me only when I wanted to breathe in your soft pain... those mice cannot be saved from me.
