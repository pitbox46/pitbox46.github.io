For Alex

January 2, 2023

The wicked work tirelessly to have easy lives The blessed work unwaiveringly to have good lives Sometimes teeth don't bite Often weary eyes will talk Sometimes gems don't shine Often the best stones cut themselves The foundations are made of evergreen, not oak The good are made in an empire of gold, not stone We are not just We are not easy We will hurt We will frown But, we will live We will be truly alive
