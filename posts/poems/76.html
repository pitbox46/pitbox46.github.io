Bonfire

February 9, 2023

The midnight owl howls well into the night The fireflies blink in an out of existence Thoughts linger around my mind like our polaroid The one that had em smiling - sleepishly - selfishly I cannot see the blinks past your burning body I cannot hear the howls beyond my crackling smile From the corner of my eye lies a shadow - roughly your size, roughly your stature I look over to that bush I shed my last tear
